Rules

Battle for Weakness is a tabletop multiplayer PvP card game, the goal of which is to defeat your opponent.

Preparation for the “battle”

Before the start of the battle, each player must choose a deck of a certain faction (at the moment these are “Mountains” and “Swamp”). For each faction, certain cards are available that are related to the selected faction in style and have a certain set of unique parameters and abilities. The choice of your strategy for the game and the gaming experience you have gained will depend on the choice of the faction
